Plan:
* The patient will be advised to take time off work to rest and reduce stress.
* The patient will be encouraged to incorporate healthier meals into their diet and to reduce their consumption of alcohol and caffeine.
* The patient will be referred to a dietician for personalized dietary advice.
* The patient will be advised to practice good sleep hygiene, including maintaining a dark, quiet, and cool bedroom, avoiding screens before bedtime, and practicing relaxation techniques.
* The patient will be monitored for any changes in their symptoms and will be re-evaluated once their test results are available.
